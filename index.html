Spanish Colors

Learn the colors in Spanish! Are you ready? Here we go!

RED === ROJO

The Spanish word for red is "Rojo".

Ariel has red hair.

Ariel tiene pelo rojo.



BLUE === AZUL

The Spanish word for blue is "Azul".

Krosh from Smeshariki has blue fur.

Krosh de Smeshariki tiene pelaje azul.



ORANGE === ANARANJADO

The Spanish word for orange is "Anaranjado".

Nami has orange hair.

Nami tiene pelo anaranjado.



GREEN === VERDE

The Spanish word for green is "Verde".

I don't like people or animals with green eyes.

No me gustan las personas o las mascotas con ojos verdes.



YELLOW === AMARILLO

The Spanish word for green is "Verde".

All #2 pencils are yellow.

Todos los lapices #2 son amarillos.
